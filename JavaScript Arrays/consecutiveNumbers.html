Q4. Given an array of positive integers. Find the length of the longest sub-sequence such that elements in the
        subsequence are consecutive integers, the consecutive numbers can be in any order.
        Input:
        N = 7
        a[] = {2,6,1,9,4,5,3}
        Output:
        6
        Explanation:The consecutive numbers here are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6. These 6 numbers form the longest consecutive
        subsquence.
